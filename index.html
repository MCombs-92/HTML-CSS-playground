We are gamers

We are a close knit group of friends who primarily enjoy the game Destiny 2 together, but we also partake in antics across other titles as well. Mainly a group of PVP appreciatives, we also dabble in the PVE side of things as well. We raid, we Nightfall, we crucible. We have fun.
